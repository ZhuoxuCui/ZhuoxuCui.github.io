Zhuo-Xu Cui

About me

  • I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Applied Mathematics at Wuhan University (WHU) in 2020 under the guidance of Professor Qibin Fan. Following that, I served as a postdoctoral researcher at the Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences, from June 2020 to August 2022, collaborating with Professor Dong Liang. Since August 2022, I have been employed at SIAT.
